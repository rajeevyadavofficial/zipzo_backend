# Zipzo Product Requirements

## Vision

Zipzo helps customers buy fresh grocery products from two trusted sources:

- **MeroMart**: Zipzo-controlled stock for quality-sensitive categories.
- **MeroDokaan**: nearby verified shops selling directly to local customers.

## MVP Goal

Launch in one delivery zone with a controlled catalog, a small group of verified shops, and a simple order flow that can be operated manually if needed.

## User Roles

- **Customer**: browses products or nearby shops and places orders.
- **Admin**: manages MeroMart, approves shops, monitors orders, and handles support.
- **Seller**: manages a verified MeroDokaan shop and its products.
- **Rider**: receives delivery assignments and updates delivery status.

## MVP Features

- Phone-based user identity model, with development login first.
- MeroMart company store catalog.
- MeroDokaan shop registration and approval.
- Seller product listing after approval.
- Customer location and nearby shop discovery.
- Single-source cart: one order comes from either MeroMart or one MeroDokaan shop.
- COD payment status.
- Order statuses: placed, accepted, packed, out for delivery, delivered, cancelled.
- Basic admin order management.

## Out Of Scope For MVP

- Multi-shop cart.
- Online payment gateway.
- Live rider map.
- Loyalty points.
- Paid shop ads.
- Alcohol delivery until legal clearance is confirmed.
- Multi-city operations.

## Launch Assumptions

- Initial launch is one zone in Kathmandu Valley.
- Cash on delivery is the first payment method.
- Products can be manually managed by admins and sellers.
- Delivery can be Zipzo-managed first, with shop-managed delivery added later.
